Why should you join us?

We are a new MC coming to the GTA5 MC community to bring in all the guys who just want have fun without having to worry about strict rules or being online all the time. I understand you have a life outside the game (I do too) so ive made a mc you dont see much anymore a true 1% mc, relaxed, and dont really care about fighting, if this sounds like something you would like give us a message we arnt too strict on joining restrictions. Just that you must be 18+ (or mature enough) Discord, Instagram, or Snapchat and prefferablly a Mic to communicate with us (at least your phone)

While we are pretty relaxed on rules there are a few things i will not stand for... Breaking 1% without other party doing so first (must record proof), Fighting amongst each other, and finally drama. When trying to join us how our prospecting works is that you join as a hangaround for at least 2 weeks, during this time we will be watching your contributions to the MC when 2 weeks have passed we will bring it up to a vote if it passes then you are assigned a Sponser whom's responsibility is to teach you the bylaws of the MC and make sure you are a good fit. This will take about another 2 weeks and then we bring it to another vote if passed you are brought to a full patch on probation of getting any officer positions (if any avaliabale) for 1 month.

Bylaws

Shoot 2nd

Don't shoot until shot at. Punishment will follow if broken. (Flashing a gun, Shooting around you etc does not count they are trying to make you shoot first)

1% until Broken

Do not break 1% until the other party does first and even then you must be commanded by highest officer present first.

Patch at all times

Wear you patch at all times only exception i allow is if you are playing with non community freinds.

Free Aim

There are 2 exceptions to this, one you are doing a mission, and two you are hanging with non community freinds

No programs

Only scumbags and people with no skill use program. If you use them in my MC you will be immediently blacklisted from the crew never to return.
